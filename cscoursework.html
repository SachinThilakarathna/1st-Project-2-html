Bachelor of Science Honours in Computer Science

Offered by the Faculty of Computing, BSc (Hons) in Computer Science degree programme is a four year programme which provides individuals with a strong foundation on theoretical concepts in the domain of Computer Science as well as the practical knowledge and skills necessary to implement new solutions and innovations as computer scientists. The Program is carefully designed in accordance with ACM/IEEE international guidelines and the requirements of Sri Lanka Qualifications Framework.


Course Duration - 4 Years
